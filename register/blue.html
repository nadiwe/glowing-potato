_CUT_OUT

_modul

ZHdK | IAD | Soft Architecture HS21
Group Work


_about

Description: Ohrbit is an interactive spatial sound installation. Consisting of a portable sphere, a pedestal and four speakers arranged in space, a unique sound experience is created. By interacting with the pedestal or rotating the sphere in space, the user can change and explore the soundscape around them.The installation is divided into two sound worlds. They are fundamentally opposed to e ach other. If the sphere is on the pedestal, the inside of the sphere is explored. If the sphere is in space, the sound space is also the thing to be explored.


_focus

- OCS connecting with Processing and abelton live
- getting an understanding how sounds effect emotions
- how to create sound interaction between space, object and person
